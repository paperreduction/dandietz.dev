Engineer & Educator

Dan Dietz – Hey there, I’m a software engineer, web developer, and educator.

I’ve been building for the web since I was a kid back in 1997 and professionally since 2008. I've built eCommerce sites, portals, and a variety of complex systems for big brands, but these days I'm a partner at BoltAffect and a developer at Grace Church. Both allow me to work with smaller local companies and ministries that are making a difference right here in Greenville, SC. I am also a teacher with a passion for education. Over the years, I've taught AP Chemistry, web engineering, and led up several corporate training programs.

Both engineering and education are reflections of my core belief that Jesus sent us into our communities to spread the gospel and bring His kingdom here on earth while we wait for His return.
